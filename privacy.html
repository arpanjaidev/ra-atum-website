Privacy Policy

Effective Date: August 27, 2025

Introduction

RA Atum values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our websites (e.g., raatumtoken.com, online-ra.com) and DApps.

1. Information We Collect

  • Voluntary Information: name, email, and messages when you contact us.
  • Automatic Information: IP address, browser type, device info, pages viewed, timestamps (via cookies/analytics).
  • Blockchain Data: wallet addresses and transaction IDs (publicly available on BSC or other networks).

2. How We Use Information

  • Operate and secure our website, DApp, and services.
  • Improve user experience and develop new features.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Sharing of Information

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We may share limited data with service providers (hosting, analytics, security) under confidentiality obligations.
  • Blockchain data is public by design and may be viewed by anyone.

4. Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures (encryption, access controls). No system is 100% secure; you use our services at your own risk.

5. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

  • Right to access, correct, or delete personal data (subject to law).
  • Right to opt-out of marketing communications.
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time for optional processing.
  • To exercise rights, contact us at support@raatumtoken.com.

6. Data Retention

We retain personal data only as long as necessary for the purposes described or as required by law. Public blockchain records may persist indefinitely.

7. Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under the age of 18. If you believe we have collected personal data from a minor, contact us to request deletion.

8. International Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries other than your own. We take steps to ensure appropriate protections consistent with applicable laws.

9. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies. For details, see our Cookie Policy. You can control cookies via your browser settings.

10. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy periodically. Material changes will be indicated by the updated Effective Date. Continued use constitutes acceptance.

11. Contact

For privacy requests, contact: support@raatumtoken.com
