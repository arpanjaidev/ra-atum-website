Who We Are

Our Team.
At RA, we are powered by innovation, compassion, and a mission to reshape how children’s fashion and technology come together. Behind the scenes, a strong team of 30 expert engineers drives our ecosystem — combining creativity, code, and care to support every branch of our brand.

From our stylish, quality kidswear and curated toys to our socially driven donation-for-reward system, our team is building more than a store — we’re building a platform for purpose.

Our engineers manage everything from smart contract development to backend infrastructure using secure VPS hosting, VPN-protected environments, and scalable cloud systems. Whether it's handling blockchain integration for RA Atum Token, or managing our secure wallet connections, automated token rewards, and presale systems, we keep our operations safe, fast, and future-ready.

We’re not just growing a brand — we’re nurturing a tech-enabled, child-focused ecosystem with ethics, security, and innovation at its heart. Every project we launch, every donor we reward, and every product we ship is a result of teamwork, precision, and passion.
