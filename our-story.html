Our Story
Founded in 2023 by crypto and web3 believers, RA Atum began as a passion project to solve two big problems:
Accessibility
Most donation and token platforms are either too complex, too expensive, or just don’t reward true kindness.
Fair Launches
Too many projects face manipulation by centralized players or big influencers. RA Atum puts fairness and equal access first.
Today, RA Atum is a global team of engineers, designers, and web3 pioneers building the first donation platform and store where:
  • Anyone can make a difference in minutes
  • Communities help shape the platform with RA Atum tokens
  • Success is measured by kindness and impact—not exploitation
