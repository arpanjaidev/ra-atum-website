Troubleshooting & Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I connect my wallet?
1. Make sure MetaMask (or your wallet app) is installed and unlocked.
2. Click "Connect Wallet" on the main page.
3. Approve the connection prompt in your wallet.
If you’re on mobile, use WalletConnect or open in the MetaMask app browser.
My RA Atum tokens aren’t showing up!
  • Check that your wallet is on Binance Smart Chain.
  • Import the token: 0xcE06aDbB070c2f0d90Ba109E77c0c2Ff83F9Ff3A
  • Click "Import Tokens" in MetaMask and paste the contract address above.
  • If you purchased tokens recently, please wait for blockchain confirmations.
I have a failed transaction. What should I do?
  • Make sure you have enough BNB for gas fees.
  • Try again with a smaller amount.
  • Clear cache, refresh, and reconnect wallet if issues continue.

Common Issues & Solutions

Wallet won’t connect?
- Ensure your browser supports wallet extensions (like Chrome).
- Reload your wallet and the site.
- Double check that you’re on the correct network.
Token balance incorrect?
- Re-sync your wallet.
- Make sure you are connected to the correct account.
- Wait a few minutes for the blockchain to update.
Having trouble buying tokens?
- Confirm you have enough BNB for the transaction and gas.
- If the issue persists, reach out to support.
