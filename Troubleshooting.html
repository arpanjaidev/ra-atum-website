Troubleshooting & Help Center
Wallet & Token Issues
  • Wallet not connecting? Make sure MetaMask or Trust Wallet is installed and connected to Binance Smart Chain. Try refreshing and reconnecting your wallet.
  • Tokens not showing? Add your RA Atum token contract address manually in your wallet and ensure you're on the right network.
  • Failed transactions? Check your gas fee, ensure you have enough BNB, and try again. Network congestion may cause delays.
Agent & Platform Issues
  • Agent not working? Check that your wallet is connected and all platform settings are saved before deploying agents or interacting with NFTs.
  • Performance errors? Refresh your browser and clear cache. If problems persist, try another device or browser.
Dashboard, Access & Analytics
  • Stats not loading? Sometimes blockchain data may take a while to update. Wait a minute and refresh.
  • Account or access issues? Double check your wallet address, internet, and browser compatibility. Disable VPN if needed.
  • Leaderboard/Referral errors? Check your referrals and ensure all qualifying transactions have been completed and confirmed.
