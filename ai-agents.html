What is RA Atum?

RA Atum is India’s first donation-powered blockchain platform where your generosity creates real-world impact and rewards you with digital assets.

Donate toys, books, or clothes, and you’ll receive RA Atum tokens instantly. Every donation is recorded on-chain, giving you total transparency and ownership over your giving journey.

RA Atum goes beyond donations – you can also shop for kids’ essentials, earn tokens, and join a global movement of kindness and innovation. The RA Atum token is your gateway to voting, claiming special rewards, accessing NFTs, and helping shape the future of our community.
